# Combat & Ship Design

### 1. Ship Classes & Loadouts
For the MVP phase, ship designs are static. Players build pre-defined hulls and vessel classes (e.g., Frigates, Cruisers, Dreadnoughts) rather than individually outfitting modular components like specialized heatsinks or drive plumes. However, the system is designed to eventually support granular, modular ship design in future expansions.

### 2. Fuel & Logistics
Currently, to facilitate smooth testing of the FTL mechanics and general maneuvers, ships operate with infinite fuel (unlimited Delta-V). In later iterations, strict fuel requirements and deep-space refueling supply chains will be introduced to complicate deep-space warfare.

### 3. Tactical Combat
* **Weaponry:** Space combat utilizes a mix of both speed-of-light directed-energy weapons (Lasers) and relativistic kinetic strike munitions. 
* **Damage Resolution:** To maintain MVP simplicity, ship integrity is abstracted into a standard "health bar" system. Later expansions may evolve this into a localized, system-based damage model where taking a hit to the engines physically cripples thrust.
