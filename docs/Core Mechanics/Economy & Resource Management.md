# Economy & Resource Management

### 1. The Core Philosophy
In *Project Galáctica*, the economy is grounded in realism. There are no "space magic" materials or overtly complex, convoluted production chains in the MVP. Resources are simple, understandable, and fundamentally essential to survival and expansion.

### 2. Primary Resource Generation
Mining is the backbone of any empire's economy. Resource extraction happens in two primary locations:
* **Planetary Extraction:** Mining operations established on colonized planets or outposts. These provide consistent yields down a gravity well but are vulnerable to siege or planetary blockades.
* **Space-Based Mining:** Harvesting from non-terrestrial bodies such as asteroid belts and meteors. This relies heavily on zero-G logistical networks and can be easily disrupted or pillaged by fast-moving enemy fleets.

### 3. Core Resources
To keep the MVP streamlined, resources are limited to simple, physical commodities. Detailed mechanics regarding specific mined resources (e.g., base metals, raw energy fuels, biological solvents) will remain grounded in realistic interstellar mining operations.

### 4. Planetary Population Management
At this stage of development it is kept intentionally super simple. You manage colonies not by placing individual infrastructure buildings on a grid, but simply by assigning **percentages of the planetary population** to specific logistical tasks (e.g., 60% of pops to mining, 40% to research). There are no complex internal unrest or civilian rebellion mechanics modeled for the MVP.
